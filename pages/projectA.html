Fallacious Animals

Logical Fallacies Learner Web App

Welcome to Fallacious Animals, the ultimate web app designed to transform your understanding of logical fallacies into a captivating and enjoyable learning experience. Picture a world where learning about reasoning errors is as engaging as exploring a zoo filled with fascinating creatures. Fallacious Animals goes beyond traditional approaches, bringing logical fallacies to life through a vibrant and interactive platform. Dive into the world of playful animals, each representing a specific fallacy, as you embark on a journey to master the art of critical thinking. Whether you're a student, professional, or just someone curious about the intricacies of sound reasoning, Fallacious Animals is here to make the exploration of logical fallacies both educational and entertaining.

What kind of project is this?

Fallacious Animals is a dynamic logical fallacies learner app that introduces three transformative ideas reshaping the understanding of software engineering. The Agile Methodology insight emphasizes iterative development, collaboration, and adaptability. Microservices architecture explores the modular and decentralized structure's impact on scalability. Inclusivity heuristics become a guiding principle, fostering ethical responsibility for creating inclusive solutions. Empowered by these insights, the app incorporates Agile practices, modular architecture, and inclusive design, offering an engaging and educational experience. As a potential microservice, an "Incorrect Answer Tracking Microservice" allows users to review and learn from their mistakes, enhancing the educational effectiveness of the app.

Quality Attributes


1. User Experience (UX)
Definition: Overall ease with which the user can interact with and navigate the app, as well as their satisfaction with the experience. Relevance: A positive UX directly impacts user engagement and retention. An intuitive interface encourages users to explore and engage with the app effortlessly.

2. Educational Effectiveness
Definition: Educational value of the app’s content and its ability to teach users how to recognize logical fallacies in public discourse. Relevance: The primary purpose of the app is to educate users about logical fallacies, making the quality and effectiveness of educational content crucial. Users turn to the app with the intention of learning, emphasizing the importance of a strong educational framework.

3. Entertainment
Definition: The app’s ability to engage and captivate users through enjoyable elements. Relevance: Keeping learners engaged and motivated is essential. Gamification, interactive elements, and enjoyable interactions create a positive and motivating learning atmosphere.
